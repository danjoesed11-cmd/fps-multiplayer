Game Modes

Six Ways to Play

Every match plays differently — from frantic team fights to tense hide-and-seek.

⚔️

Team Deathmatch

Two teams race to 30 kills. The most kills take the win.

🚩

Capture the Flag

Grab the enemy flag, return it to your base. First to 3 captures wins.

🏆

Zone Wars

Capture and hold control zones to rack up points. Hold more, score faster.

🫥

Hide & Seek

Hiders blend into the map. Seekers hunt them before time runs out.

💀

Team Wipeout

No respawns — eliminate the entire enemy team. Win 3 rounds to win the match.

🤖

Singleplayer

Fight AI bots with full economy and upgrade access. Perfect for practice.

Systems

Everything You Need

Every system is built to talk to the others — kill enemies, earn coins, buy upgrades, repeat.

🌐

Real-Time Multiplayer

ENet/WebSocket networking with server-authoritative hit validation. Up to 16 players.

💰

Economy & Shop

Kill enemies to earn coins. Visit buy zones between rounds to upgrade or buy new weapons.

💬

Lobby & Chat

Server-relayed text chat, ready-up system, and mode/map voting before every match.

🎨

Character Customization

Equip skins for body, head, gloves, and boots. Changes replicate to all players live.

🤖

AI Bots

Target-finding bots with patrol, chase, and attack states. Full singleplayer support.
